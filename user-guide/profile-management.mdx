# Profile Management

Your profile page is your central hub for managing personal information, viewing enrolled conferences, and tracking your participation in CONIITI 2026.

## Accessing Your Profile

Navigate to your profile page at `/perfil` or click on the profile link in the navigation menu after logging in.

<Note>
  You must be logged in to access your profile. If you're not authenticated, you'll be redirected to the login page.
</Note>

## Profile Overview

Your profile page consists of three main sections:

1. **Personal Data** (Mis Datos Personales)
2. **Enrolled Conferences** (Mis Conferencias Inscritas)
3. **Attendance History** (Certificados de Asistencia)

---

## Managing Personal Data

### Viewing Your Information

Your profile displays:
- **Full Name** (Nombre Completo)
- **Email Address** (Correo Electrónico) - Cannot be changed
- **Role** (Rol) - Student, Professor, or Guest
- **Document Number** (Número de Documento) - For students and professors
- **Institutional Code** (Código Institucional) - Students only
- **Career** (Carrera) - Students only
- **Gender** - Selected during registration

### Editing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Edit Mode">
    Click the **"Editar Perfil"** (Edit Profile) button in the top-right corner of the Personal Data section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Editable Fields">
    You can modify:
    - **Full Name**: Update your name if needed
    - **Career** (Students only): Change your academic program

    <Note>
      Email, role, document number, and institutional code cannot be changed for security and data integrity reasons.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **"Guardar"** (Save) to save your changes.
    
    Your updates are saved both:
    - Locally in your browser (for offline access)
    - On the server (when connected)

    <Tip>
      You'll see a confirmation message once your changes are successfully saved.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Password Management

Your profile includes a dedicated security section for changing your password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Security Section">
    Scroll down to the **"🔒 Seguridad - Cambiar Contraseña"** section in your profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Fill in the following fields:
    - **Nueva Contraseña** (New Password)
    - **Confirmar Nueva Contraseña** (Confirm New Password)

    <Note>
      Password requirements:
      - Minimum 6 characters
      - Both password fields must match
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Password">
    Click **"Actualizar Contraseña"** (Update Password) to save your new password.
    
    You'll receive a confirmation message when the password is successfully updated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Your password is updated locally and will be used for your next login. Make sure to remember your new password!
</Tip>

## Enrolled Conferences Section

This section displays all conferences you've registered for that haven't been attended yet.

### Conference Card Information

Each conference card shows:
- 📅 **Conference Title**
- **Day and Time**: Day number, start time, and end time
- 📍 **Location**: Physical location or "Plataforma Virtual" for online conferences
- 🔗 **Virtual Meeting Link**: For virtual conferences, click "Unirse a la Reunión" to join

### Managing Your Enrollments

#### Canceling an Enrollment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Conference">
    Locate the conference you want to cancel in the "Mis Conferencias Inscritas" section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Cancel">
    Click the **"Cancelar"** button on the conference card.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Confirm your decision in the popup dialog.
    
    <Note>
      Canceling your enrollment will:
      - Remove the conference from your enrolled list
      - Free up your spot for other attendees
      - Cannot be easily undone - you'll need to re-enroll if spots are still available
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Joining Virtual Conferences

For conferences marked as virtual (🌐 Virtual):
1. Click the **"🔗 Unirse a la Reunión"** (Join Meeting) link
2. You'll be redirected to the virtual conference platform
3. Follow the platform's instructions to join

<Tip>
  Join virtual conferences a few minutes early to ensure your connection is working properly.
</Tip>

#### No Conferences Yet?

If you see "No tienes conferencias pendientes" (No pending conferences), visit the Agenda page to browse and enroll in available conferences.

## Attendance History (Certificados de Asistencia)

This section displays conferences you've successfully attended and had your presence confirmed via QR code scanning.

### What You'll See

For each completed conference:
- ✅ **Completion Badge**: Green checkmark indicator
- **Conference Title**: With green highlight
- **Date and Time**: When the conference took place
- **Location**: Where the conference was held
- **Status**: "Confirmada presencialmente por Escáner QR" (Confirmed in person via QR Scanner)
- **COMPLETADO Badge**: Visual completion indicator

<Note>
  Conferences only appear in this section after you've scanned the QR code at the event. See the [QR Attendance](/user-guide/qr-attendance) guide for more details.
</Note>

### Empty History

If you see "Todavía no has asistido a ninguna conferencia" (You haven't attended any conferences yet), it means:
- You haven't attended any enrolled conferences yet, or
- You haven't scanned the attendance QR code at the conferences you attended

## Real-Time Synchronization

Your profile data synchronizes across browser tabs automatically:
- Changes in one tab reflect immediately in other open tabs
- Logout in one tab logs you out everywhere
- Conference enrollments and cancellations update instantly

## Offline Functionality

<Note>
  Your profile works in offline mode:
  - Changes are saved locally in your browser
  - Updates sync to the server when you reconnect
  - You can view your data even without internet connection
</Note>

## Profile Data Privacy

Your profile information is:
- Stored securely on the server
- Accessible only to you and authorized administrators
- Used exclusively for CONIITI 2026 event management
- Protected under Colombian data protection laws

## Troubleshooting

### Profile Not Loading

**Problem**: You're redirected to login when accessing `/perfil`

**Solution**: 
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check if your session expired
- Try logging in again

### Changes Not Saving

**Problem**: Profile updates don't persist

**Solution**:
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you clicked "Guardar" (Save)
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if the issue persists

### Missing Conference Information

**Problem**: Enrolled conferences don't appear

**Solution**:
- Verify you're logged in with the correct account
- Check the Agenda page to confirm your enrollments
- Try refreshing the page
