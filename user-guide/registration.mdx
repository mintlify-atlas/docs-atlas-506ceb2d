# User Registration

Welcome to CONIITI 2026! This guide will walk you through the process of creating your account to access all conference features.

## Getting Started

To register for CONIITI 2026, navigate to the registration page from the home page or go directly to `/registro`.

## Registration Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Role">
    Choose the role that best describes you:
    - **Student (Estudiante)**: University students attending the conference
    - **Professor (Profesor)**: Academic faculty and educators
    - **Guest (Invitado)**: External participants and industry professionals

    <Note>
      Your role selection determines which additional information you'll need to provide.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Basic Information">
    Fill in your essential details:
    - **Full Name** (Nombres y Apellidos Completos)
    - **Email Address** (Correo Electrónico)
    - **Password** (Contraseña)

    <Tip>
      Use a valid email address - you'll need it to log in and receive conference updates.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide Identification Details">
    Select your document type and enter your ID number:
    - **Document Type**: Choose between:
      - CC (Cédula de Ciudadanía)
      - CE (Cédula de Extranjería)
    - **Document Number**: Enter your identification number
  </Step>

  <Step title="Student-Specific Information" icon="graduation-cap">
    If you selected **Student**, you'll need to provide:
    - **Institutional Code** (Código): Your university student ID
    - **Career** (Carrera): Select your program from the dropdown:
      - Administración de Empresas
      - Arquitectura
      - Derecho
      - Economía
      - Ingeniería Civil
      - Ingeniería de Sistemas
      - Ingeniería Electrónica
      - Ingeniería Industrial
      - Psicología

    <Note>
      The institutional code must be unique - each code can only be registered once.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Gender">
    Choose your gender from the dropdown menu:
    - Masculino (Male)
    - Femenino (Female)
    - Otro (Other)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Accept Terms and Conditions">
    Review and accept the data privacy policy:
    - Check the box to accept the **política de tratamiento de datos personales** (personal data processing policy)
    - Click the policy link to view the full details

    <Note>
      Your data will be used exclusively for academic purposes and CONIITI 2026 event management in accordance with Law 1581 of 2012 (Colombian data protection law).
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Click the **"Registrarse"** (Register) button to create your account.
    
    Once registered, you'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your profile page where you can:
    - Update your information
    - Enroll in conferences
    - Track your conference attendance
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Registration

Once you've successfully registered:

✅ **Automatic Login**: You'll be logged in immediately after registration  
✅ **Profile Access**: View and edit your profile at `/perfil`  
✅ **Conference Enrollment**: Browse and enroll in available conferences  
✅ **QR Code Access**: Use QR scanning for attendance tracking  

## Common Registration Issues

### "Document number already registered"

This means an account with your ID number already exists. Try logging in instead of registering.

### "Institutional code already registered"

For students: Each institutional code can only be used once. Verify your student ID or contact support if you believe this is an error.

### Email Format Error

Ensure your email follows the standard format: `username@domain.com`

## Already Have an Account?

If you already registered, click **"¿Ya tienes una cuenta? Inicia sesión"** (Already have an account? Login here) at the bottom of the registration form to access the login page.

## Language Support
