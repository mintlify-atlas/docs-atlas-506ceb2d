# QR Code Attendance

CONIITI 2026 uses QR code technology to track attendance at in-person conferences and generate attendance certificates. This guide explains how to scan QR codes and verify your participation.

## Why QR Code Attendance?

QR code scanning provides:
- ✅ **Instant verification**: Confirm your attendance in seconds
- 📜 **Certificate eligibility**: Attendance records enable certificate generation
- 📊 **Accurate tracking**: Helps organizers understand conference participation
- 🎯 **Proof of completion**: Documented evidence of your conference participation

<Note>
  QR code scanning is **only required for in-person conferences** (📍 Presencial). Virtual conferences (🌐 Virtual) are tracked automatically through the online platform.
</Note>

---

## Prerequisites

Before scanning QR codes:

1. **Active Enrollment**: You must be enrolled in the conference (see [Conference Enrollment](/user-guide/conference-enrollment))
2. **Physical Presence**: Attend the conference in person
3. **Logged In**: Be signed into your CONIITI 2026 account
4. **Camera Access**: Grant your browser permission to use your device's camera

---

## Accessing the QR Scanner

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Profile">
    Navigate to your profile page at `/perfil` or click your profile link in the navigation menu.
    
    <Note>
      You must be logged in to access the QR scanner. If you're not authenticated, you'll be redirected to the login page.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Locate Scanner Button">
    In the **"Mis Conferencias Inscritas"** (My Enrolled Conferences) section, find the button:
    
    **📷 Escanear QR Asistencia** (Scan Attendance QR)
    
    This button is located in the top-right area of the enrolled conferences section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Scanner">
    Click the **"📷 Escanear QR Asistencia"** button to launch the QR code scanner.
    
    <Tip>
      The scanner opens in a modal overlay, so you can easily close it if needed without losing your place.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Scanning a QR Code

Once the scanner is open:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grant Camera Permission">
    Your browser will request permission to access your camera:
    - Click **"Allow"** or **"Permitir"**
    - This permission is only for the CONIITI website
    - You can revoke it later in your browser settings

    <Note>
      If you previously denied camera access, you'll need to manually enable it in your browser settings:
      - **Chrome/Edge**: Click the padlock icon in the address bar → Site settings → Camera → Allow
      - **Firefox**: Click the shield icon → Permissions → Camera → Allow
      - **Safari**: Safari → Settings → Websites → Camera → Allow for this website
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Position Your Camera">
    The scanner displays a live camera view:
    - Hold your device steady
    - Position the camera so the QR code fits within the view
    - Ensure good lighting for best results
    - The QR code will be automatically detected when in focus

    <Tip>
      Keep your device at a comfortable distance (15-30 cm from the QR code) for optimal scanning.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Align with QR Code">
    Point your camera at the conference attendance QR code:
    - The QR code is typically displayed:
      - On screens at the conference venue
      - On printed posters at the entrance
      - On the speaker's presentation slides
    - Center the QR code in your camera view

    <Note>
      Each conference has a unique QR code. Make sure you're scanning the code for the correct conference!
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automatic Detection">
    The scanner automatically detects and processes the QR code:
    - No need to press any buttons
    - Processing happens instantly upon detection
    - The scanner validates the code against your enrollments

    <Tip>
      If the scan doesn't work immediately, try adjusting your angle or moving slightly closer/farther from the code.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Scanning

### Successful Scan

When you successfully scan an attendance QR code:

1. **Scanner Closes**: The QR scanner modal automatically closes
2. **Success Message**: You'll see a confirmation message:
   - "¡Asistencia registrada con éxito! Ya puedes ver tu certificado en el historial."
   - (Attendance successfully registered! You can now view your certificate in history.)
   - Message displayed with a **green background**
3. **Automatic Update**: The scanned conference moves from "Enrolled" to "Attendance History"
4. **Page Refresh**: Your profile updates in real-time

<Note>
  The success message disappears automatically after 4 seconds, or you can continue using your profile immediately.
</Note>

### Invalid QR Code

If you scan an invalid or unrecognized QR code:

1. **Scanner Closes**: The scanner automatically closes
2. **Error Message**: You'll see:
   - "Código QR no válido para asistencia."
   - (QR code not valid for attendance.)
   - Message displayed with a **red background**
3. **No Changes**: Your enrollment status remains unchanged

**Common reasons for invalid codes:**
- QR code is not a CONIITI attendance code
- QR code is for a different conference
- QR code is expired or inactive
- QR code format is incorrect

<Tip>
  If you consistently get invalid code errors, verify with event staff that you're scanning the correct conference's QR code.
</Tip>

## Understanding QR Code Format

For technical reference, CONIITI attendance QR codes follow this format:

```
CONF_ATTENDANCE_[conference_id]
```

For example:
- `CONF_ATTENDANCE_1`
- `CONF_ATTENDANCE_42`
- `CONF_ATTENDANCE_305`

<Note>
  The system automatically validates that:
  - The QR code format is correct
  - The conference ID matches one of your enrollments
  - You haven't already scanned this code
</Note>

## Attendance History Management

### Viewing Your History

Your attendance history section shows:
- **All successfully scanned conferences**: In chronological order
- **Permanent records**: Cannot be deleted or modified
- **Complete details**: Title, date, time, location, and confirmation status

### Empty History

If you see:
- "Todavía no has asistido a ninguna conferencia."
- (You haven't attended any conferences yet.)

This means you haven't scanned any QR codes yet. Enroll in conferences and scan the QR codes at the events to build your attendance record.

### Data Persistence

Your attendance records are:
- **Stored permanently** on the server
- **Synced across devices** when you log in
- **Available offline** for viewing
- **Used for certificate generation** at the end of CONIITI 2026

## Troubleshooting

### Scanner Won't Open

**Problem**: Clicking the scan button doesn't open the scanner

**Solutions**:
1. Verify you're logged in
2. Refresh the page and try again
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Try a different browser

### Camera Not Working

**Problem**: Black screen or "No camera access" message

**Solutions**:
1. **Check browser permissions**: Allow camera access in site settings
2. **Check device permissions**: Ensure your OS allows the browser to use the camera
3. **Close other apps**: Other applications using the camera may block access
4. **Try another device**: Use a different phone or tablet
5. **Check camera hardware**: Test your camera in another app to ensure it works

### QR Code Won't Scan

**Problem**: Scanner doesn't detect the QR code

**Solutions**:
1. **Improve lighting**: Ensure the QR code is well-lit
2. **Reduce glare**: Avoid reflections on screens or laminated prints
3. **Adjust distance**: Move closer or farther from the code
4. **Hold steady**: Keep your device still while scanning
5. **Clean camera lens**: Wipe your camera lens if it's dirty
6. **Check QR code quality**: Ensure the code isn't damaged or blurry

### Wrong Conference Recorded

**Problem**: Scanned the wrong conference's QR code

**Solution**: 
- Contact event organizers immediately
- Provide your email and the conference details
- Staff can manually adjust attendance records

<Note>
  Be careful to scan only the QR code for the conference you're currently attending!
</Note>

### Attendance Not Showing

**Problem**: Scanned successfully but conference doesn't appear in history

**Solutions**:
1. Wait a few moments - updates may take time
2. Refresh your profile page
3. Log out and log back in
4. Check if you're logged into the correct account
5. Contact support if the issue persists

## Canceling the Scanner

If you need to close the scanner without scanning:

1. Click the **"Cancelar"** (Cancel) button at the bottom of the scanner modal
2. The scanner closes and returns you to your profile
3. No changes are made to your attendance records

<Tip>
  You can open and close the scanner as many times as needed without any penalty.
</Tip>

## Best Practices

### When to Scan

- ✅ **Scan at the event**: Ideally at the start or end of the conference
- ✅ **Scan while present**: Only scan when physically attending
- ✅ **Scan once**: Each conference only needs to be scanned once
- ❌ **Don't scan remotely**: Don't scan QR codes from photos or screenshots
- ❌ **Don't scan for others**: Each attendee must scan their own code

### Technical Tips

- Keep your device charged or bring a power bank
- Ensure your browser is up to date
- Test the scanner before the first conference
- Screenshot your enrolled conferences for offline reference

### Privacy Considerations

- Camera access is only used for QR scanning
- No photos or videos are recorded or stored
- Camera permission is specific to the CONIITI website
- You can revoke camera access anytime in browser settings

## Attendance and Certificates

Your scanned attendance records determine:

### Certificate Eligibility
- **Participation certificate**: Issued based on total conferences attended
- **Individual conference certificates**: Generated for each confirmed attendance
- **Course completion**: May require minimum attendance threshold

### How Certificates Are Generated

At the end of CONIITI 2026:
1. Organizers review all attendance records
2. Certificates are automatically generated
3. Certificates include:
   - Your name and ID
   - Conferences attended
   - Total participation hours
   - Official CONIITI seal
4. Certificates are available for download in your profile

<Note>
  Certificate generation typically occurs 1-2 weeks after the event concludes. You'll be notified via email when certificates are ready.
</Note>
