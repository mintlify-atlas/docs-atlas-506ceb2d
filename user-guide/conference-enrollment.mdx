# Conference Enrollment

The Agenda page is where you'll discover all available conferences, filter by your interests, and enroll in the sessions you want to attend.

## Accessing the Agenda

Navigate to the Agenda page at `/agenda` or click the Agenda link in the navigation menu.

<Note>
  You can browse conferences without logging in, but you must be authenticated to enroll in conferences.
</Note>

---

## Browsing Conferences

### Conference Day Navigation

CONIITI 2026 spans multiple days. Use the day tabs at the top of the Agenda page to switch between:
- **Día 1** (Day 1)
- **Día 2** (Day 2)
- **Día 3** (Day 3)

<Tip>
  Click on any day tab to see only the conferences scheduled for that day.
</Tip>

### Understanding Conference Cards

Each conference is displayed as a card containing:

#### Header Information
- **Modality Badge** (Top-right corner):
  - 🌐 **Virtual**: Online conference
  - 📍 **Presencial**: In-person conference
- **Category Badge**: Conference category (e.g., "General")

#### Conference Details
- **Title**: Main conference name in blue
- **Description**: Brief overview of the conference content
- **Time**: 🕒 Start and end times
- **Location**: 📍 Physical location or "Plataforma Virtual"
- **Speaker**: 👤 Presenter name

#### Availability Information
- **Disponibilidad** (Availability) section with:
  - Current available seats vs. total capacity
  - Visual progress bar indicating seat availability
  - Color-coded background:
    - 🟢 **Green**: Many seats available (100-150 seats)
    - 🟡 **Yellow**: Moderate availability (50-100 seats)
    - 🟠 **Orange**: Limited seats (1-50 seats)
    - 🔴 **Red**: No seats available (Agotado)

#### Additional Resources
Some conferences include:
- 🔗 **Enlace al Material**: Link to external conference materials
- 📁 **Descargar Documento**: Downloadable conference documents

## Filtering and Searching

### Search Bar

Use the search box at the top of the page to find specific conferences:
- Search by **conference title**
- Search by **speaker name**
- Results update in real-time as you type

<Tip>
  The search is case-insensitive, so you can type in uppercase or lowercase.
</Tip>

### Filter by Modality

Use the **📍 Modalidad** (Modality) dropdown to filter:
- **Todas las Modalidades**: Show all conferences (default)
- **Presencial**: Only in-person conferences
- **Virtual**: Only online conferences

### Filter by Career

Use the **🎓 Carrera** (Career) dropdown to show conferences relevant to your field:
- **Todas las Carreras**: Show all conferences (default)
- **Administración de Empresas**
- **Arquitectura**
- **Derecho**
- **Economía**
- **Ingeniería Civil**
- **Ingeniería de Sistemas**
- **Ingeniería Electrónica**
- **Ingeniería Industrial**
- **Psicología**
- **Diseño Gráfico**
- **General**: Conferences open to all majors

<Note>
  Filters work together - you can combine search, modality, and career filters to narrow down your options.
</Note>

### Clearing Filters

When you have active filters, a **"✕ Limpiar"** (Clear) button appears:
- Click it to reset all filters
- Returns you to viewing all conferences for the selected day

### Results Counter

Below the filters, you'll see a count of how many conferences match your current filters:
- "X conferencias encontradas" (X conferences found)
- Updates dynamically as you adjust filters

## Enrolling in a Conference

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Conference">
    Use the day tabs, search, and filters to locate the conference you want to attend.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Availability">
    Review the **Disponibilidad** section:
    - Ensure there are available seats
    - Note the conference time to avoid schedule conflicts
    - Check if it's virtual or in-person

    <Note>
      If you see "Agotado" (Sold Out), the conference is full and enrollment is not available.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Enroll">
    Click the blue **"Inscribirse ahora"** (Enroll now) button on the conference card.

    <Tip>
      The button is prominently displayed in the center of each conference card.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    You'll see "Procesando..." (Processing...) while your enrollment is being registered.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Success!">
    Once enrolled:
    - You'll see a success message: "🎉 ¡Inscripción exitosa! Puedes verla en tu perfil."
    - The button changes to **"✓ Inscrito"** (Enrolled) with a gray background
    - Available seats decrease by one
    - The conference appears in your profile
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Enrollment Without Login

If you try to enroll without being logged in:
1. You'll see an alert: "Debes iniciar sesión para inscribirte en conferencias."
2. You'll be redirected to the login page
3. After logging in, return to the Agenda to enroll

## Managing Your Enrollments

### Viewing Enrolled Conferences

After enrolling:
- Go to your **Profile** page (`/perfil`)
- View all enrolled conferences in the "Mis Conferencias Inscritas" section
- See details including day, time, and location
- Access virtual meeting links for online conferences

### Identifying Enrolled Conferences

On the Agenda page, conferences you're already enrolled in show:
- **✓ Inscrito** button instead of "Inscribirse ahora"
- Gray button background (not clickable)
- You cannot enroll twice in the same conference

### Canceling Enrollment

To cancel an enrollment:
1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Find the conference in "Mis Conferencias Inscritas"
3. Click **"Cancelar"**
4. Confirm the cancellation
5. The conference will be removed from your enrolled list
6. The seat becomes available for others

<Note>
  You can re-enroll in a conference after canceling if seats are still available.
</Note>

## Conference Capacity System

### How Capacity Works

- **Virtual Conferences**: 500 maximum attendees
- **In-Person Conferences**: Varies by location (typically 150 seats)
- Real-time seat tracking
- First-come, first-served enrollment

### Capacity Indicators

The visual progress bar and background color help you quickly assess availability:

- **Green background** (100-150 available): Plenty of space
- **Yellow background** (50-100 available): Moderate availability
- **Orange background** (1-50 available): Limited seats - enroll soon!
- **Red background** (0 available): Conference is full

<Tip>
  Popular conferences fill up quickly! Enroll early to secure your spot.
</Tip>

### Full Conferences

When a conference reaches capacity:
- Button displays **"Cupos Agotados"** (Seats Full)
- Button is disabled (grayed out)
- Progress bar shows 0% availability
- Red background in the availability section

## Virtual vs. In-Person Conferences

### Virtual Conferences (🌐 Virtual)

**Characteristics:**
- Attended online via video conferencing platform
- Higher capacity (500 seats)
- Location shows "Plataforma Virtual"
- Includes **"🔗 Unirse a la Reunión"** (Join Meeting) link in your profile

**How to Attend:**
1. Enroll in the conference
2. Go to your Profile page
3. Find the conference in "Mis Conferencias Inscritas"
4. Click "🔗 Unirse a la Reunión" at the scheduled time
5. Join the virtual conference platform

### In-Person Conferences (📍 Presencial)

**Characteristics:**
- Attended physically at a specific location
- Capacity varies by venue (typically 150 seats)
- Location shows the physical room or building
- Requires QR code scanning for attendance confirmation

**How to Attend:**
1. Enroll in the conference
2. Note the location and time
3. Arrive at the venue at the scheduled time
4. Scan the QR code displayed at the event
5. Your attendance will be recorded

<Note>
  See the [QR Attendance](/user-guide/qr-attendance) guide for detailed instructions on scanning QR codes.
</Note>

## Best Practices for Enrollment

### Plan Your Schedule
- Browse all days to avoid time conflicts
- Check conference start and end times
- Consider travel time between in-person venues
- Note whether conferences are virtual or in-person

### Enroll Early
- Popular conferences fill up quickly
- Enroll as soon as registration opens
- Monitor the availability indicators

### Diversify Your Experience
- Mix virtual and in-person conferences
- Attend conferences across different careers and topics
- Take advantage of general sessions open to all majors

### Manage Your Commitments
- Only enroll in conferences you plan to attend
- Cancel enrollments if your plans change
- Free up spots for others when you can't attend

## Troubleshooting

### "No conferences found" Message

**Cause**: Your current filters are too restrictive

**Solution**:
- Click **"✕ Limpiar"** to reset filters
- Try broader search terms
- Switch to a different day

### Unable to Enroll

**Possible causes and solutions**:

1. **Not logged in**: Log in at `/login`
2. **Conference is full**: Look for alternative conferences or check back for cancellations
3. **Already enrolled**: Check your profile to confirm
4. **Technical issue**: Refresh the page and try again

### Conference Doesn't Appear in Profile

**Solution**:
- Wait a few moments and refresh your profile page
- Verify the enrollment was successful (check for success message)
- Ensure you're logged in with the correct account

### Availability Shows Incorrect Numbers

**Solution**:
- Refresh the page to get updated seat counts
- Check the timestamp to ensure you're seeing current data
